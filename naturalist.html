A daily nature diary for the village beneath Blencathra, written each morning from the valley's own sensors — birds, bats, weather, river and sky — and gathered here into the seasons' longer story.

The Record

The valley's daily diary, with the first appearance of each species marked through the year — the returning summer visitors and the order in which Threlkeld's wildlife announces itself, woven into the days as they pass.

Each morning's entry is written automatically from the previous day's readings. Monthly and seasonal reviews look back across those entries to trace how the year unfolds in Threlkeld. The record grows richer with every passing season.
