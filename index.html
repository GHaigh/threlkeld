Local Tech & Eco Projects

A small collection of community-minded projects from a garden in Threlkeld, nestled beneath Blencathra in the northern Lake District. Each project uses locally-run technology to monitor, record and share what's happening in our little corner of Cumbria — from the birds singing at dawn to the weather rolling in off the fells.

All data is collected and processed locally with no reliance on commercial cloud services. Suggestions for new projects are always welcome.
