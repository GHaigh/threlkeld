Getting started
Up and running in 10 minutes
1
Mount the box
Find a suitable spot — 2m+ high for bird boxes, 3m+ for bat boxes, ideally facing south or south-west away from direct afternoon sun. Fix the included stainless bracket to a tree, wall or post.
2
Power it up
Bird box: the solar panel charges automatically, nothing to do. Bat box: plug the included 2m USB cable into any outdoor or indoor USB socket.
3
Connect to the box hotspot
On your phone, go to WiFi settings and connect to "ThrelkeldBirdBox" or "ThrelkeldBatBox". A setup page opens automatically — just like connecting to hotel WiFi.
4
Enter your WiFi details
Type your home WiFi name and password into the setup page. The box reboots, connects to your network, and you're done. Your credentials never leave your home network.
5
Open the live view
Visit nestbox.local in any browser on your home network. The live camera feed loads instantly. Bookmark it on your phone for one-tap access.

Questions
Does it need an internet connection?
No — the camera feed works entirely on your home WiFi network. You don't need an internet connection to view it. You do need WiFi to set it up initially.
Can I view it when I'm away from home?
Not by default — it's a local network device for simplicity and privacy. If you have a VPN into your home network you can access it remotely. We're working on an optional cloud relay feature.
What happens in winter when there are no birds?
The box just ticks over quietly. Bird boxes are often used as winter roosts too. Bat boxes are occupied from April to October. The camera is always live if you want to check.
Will it disturb the animals?
The IR LEDs are the key — the bird box uses 850nm which is effectively invisible to birds. The bat box uses 940nm which is completely invisible to bats. Neither produces any heat or audible noise.
Do you deliver outside Cumbria?
Yes — free delivery in Cumbria, £8 standard post to mainland UK. Each box is packed carefully and shipped fully assembled and tested.
Can I get a custom hole size or species variant?
Yes — just mention it when you order. We can make Robin open-fronted boxes, Treecreeper crevice boxes, or Tawny Owl boxes. Drop us an email to discuss.
