Red Squirrel
Vulnerable · Schedule 5 protected · Cumbria stronghold
The Lake District is one of the last strongholds for red squirrels in England. Threlkeld sits within the Ullswater Valley Red Squirrel Conservation Zone. Any sighting near the village is significant — please record it on iNaturalist.
